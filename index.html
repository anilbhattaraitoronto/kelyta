About Kayleen

Kayleen Trajtman is the creative entrepreneur behind Kelyta's Creation. Kayleen started to launch her children's clothing line in Canada at St Mary's Parish in Barrie. Father Sheehy and the patrons of the church allowed her to display communion dresses at church after mass.

People at church loved her beautiful designs and from that blessed beginning she worked hard to promote Kelyta's Creation and two years later she went into the wholesale business and then in 2006 she launched her retail stores and the rest is now part of her successful history.

Kelyta's Creation is a fourth generation family business and a one stop shop for all your bridal gowns, bridesmaids and mother of the bride dresses, flower girl dresses, first communion gowns, baptism gowns, boys suits, tuxedos, prom and graduation dresses. We also have a new line of Quinceañera dresses. We specialize in destination weddings and plus size gowns. All sizes can be ordered and will take approximately 60 days to arrive.

I personally travel to Los Angeles to hand pick the latest style's from designers themselves. Fashion has always been my passion and I am now bringing the latest formal wear styles to Simcoe County. I take pride in making your dreams come true..

For any alterations and fixes for your garments and dresses, please inquire at our store as we will do our best to accommodate your request. Alterations can be done within a week for customers who come from out of town or need a rushed order.

For your convenience, we offer appointments for our local clients and clients who are travelling from a distance to our shop. Please stop by for the shopping experience of your life to fully enjoy the special moments of selecting your formal wear with Kelyta.
